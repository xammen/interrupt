**`TaskScheduler` class:**
- `add_task(task, coroutine_factory)` — registers a task with a zero-arg factory (needed so retries can create a fresh coroutine).
- `get_execution_plan()` — returns `List[List[str]]` of concurrent groups via Kahn's topological sort; raises `CircularDependencyError` on cycles, `TaskNotFoundError` on unknown deps.

- **Observer pattern** — `on_task_start`, `on_task_complete`, `on_task_fail` accept async callbacks via `_EventBus`.
- **Metrics** — `TaskMetrics` per task (start/end times, elapsed, retry count) plus `total_elapsed` on the scheduler.
